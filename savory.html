Savory Foods

This is where the groove gets hearty. Our savory menu is built like a solid album — each dish carefully layered, rich with texture, and tuned to keep you fueled from the first note to the last bite. From bold breakfast sandwiches and buttery toasts to delicate egg bites and satisfying sides, every item is made to hit the spot without missing a beat. Whether you're here for a solo snack or a full-on flavor session, there's a plate playing just your tune. Hot, handheld, fork-and-knife, or straight from the skillet — these are the tracks that stick with you.
